GarbageCalendar

Garbage calendar for Ghent based on open data (data.gent.be)

Download .zip App on PlayStore View on GitHub

GarbageCalendar for Android gives you a nice overview of the garbage collections in your street (currently only in Ghent).
It also reminds you to put out the trash the night before a collection (beta).

This app uses open data from data.gent.be.
